Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum is structured to provide comprehensive knowledge and hands-on experience for students across all branches of engineering. Explore the curriculum for each branch below.

Computer Science Engineering (CSE)

Semester 1 & 2

  • Programming for Problem Solving
  • Engineering Mathematics - I & II
  • Basic Electrical & Electronics Engineering
  • Engineering Physics / Chemistry

Semester 3 & 4

  • Data Structures
  • Database Management Systems
  • Computer Organization
  • Discrete Mathematics

Semester 5 & 6

  • Operating Systems
  • Computer Networks
  • Design and Analysis of Algorithms
  • Artificial Intelligence

Semester 7 & 8

  • Machine Learning
  • Cybersecurity
  • Software Engineering
  • Major Project & Internship

Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)

Semester 1 & 2

  • Engineering Mathematics - I & II
  • Engineering Physics / Chemistry
  • Electronic Devices and Circuits
  • Basic Electrical Engineering

Semester 3 & 4

  • Digital Electronics
  • Signals and Systems
  • Control Systems
  • Analog Communication

Semester 5 & 6

  • Microprocessors and Microcontrollers
  • Digital Signal Processing
  • Embedded Systems
  • Wireless Communication

Semester 7 & 8

  • VLSI Design
  • IoT Systems
  • Advanced Communication Systems
  • Major Project & Internship

Mechanical Engineering (ME)

Semester 1 & 2

  • Engineering Mathematics - I & II
  • Engineering Physics / Chemistry
  • Engineering Mechanics
  • Basic Electrical & Electronics Engineering

Semester 3 & 4

  • Thermodynamics
  • Fluid Mechanics
  • Strength of Materials
  • Manufacturing Processes

Semester 5 & 6

  • Heat Transfer
  • Machine Design
  • Mechatronics
  • Industrial Engineering

Semester 7 & 8

  • Automobile Engineering
  • Robotics
  • Renewable Energy Systems
  • Major Project & Internship
